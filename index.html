Wholeness is possible

A Most Practical Guide To Recovery

  • An excellent recovery guide for anyone who has suffered a traumatic injury or physical crisis, and are left wondering how to move forward with life.
  • 4 components of wholeness are laid out. This book reveals why no one is beyond hope. Inspires hope in the face of hopelessness.
  • An experienced quadriplegic reveals why he has lived a long and healthy life. Here’s an insider’s perspective into the world of specific challenges that paralytics face.
  • 5 ready to implement adjustment-principles for becoming a whole person as a paralytic or any other traumatized person.
  • 16 experience-based wisdom tips for living a wholesome productive life as a paralytic or when challenged in other ways.
